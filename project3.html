Savikalpa Academy

An app aimed to improve access to different genres and categories rich in journals, literature, and books to aid reading and learning, placing as runner-up for Ada’s Base ProtoJam 2022 competition.

Challenge

Savikalpa is a pre-existing company that has its established brand identity and its cannabis business. The client showed us the content that had been prepared for the site, and there were around twenty documents, each around three to four pages. The challenge with this site is to distinct its Academy identity from its other parent sites, while still maintaining its brand identity, and keeping the amount of information legible and easy to access.

Solution

The Savikalpa Academy website acts as a database for both professionals and non-professionals. The design system introduces new icons while maintaining the colour and typography system from its parent sites. The interface offers easy access to previous and next content, allowing users to easily navigate the information-heavy site.

Navigation

Resources

Our goal was to keep the user engaged and motivated while navigating through the app and create a welcoming community for newcomers. Learning is fun! And we wanted to make sure that idea was translated through the screen. A timer? A level status? We’ve got it covered! We included a points system to help users feel accomplished when they completed a task, whether that be reading for a couple of minutes, finishing a book, or posting something in the discussion tab.
